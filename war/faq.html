Frequently Asked Questions

To resolve all your doubts/queries related to iFM so that you have a nice experience with us!

Sign Up with your IIIT-D Mail

Q. What does iFM means?


Ans. iFM stands for IIIT-D Finance Management.

Q. Do you allow online transactions?


Ans. No. This site is for monitoring your expense. You can't do any transactions here.

Q. What all transactions do you show?


Ans. Every transaction between you and the institute.

Q. Will it show my canteen expense too?


Ans. Sorry, but canteen's money is not handled by the institute. So, those transactions won't be shown here.

Q. Is this site Free or Paid?


Ans. It's absolutely free. Enjoy the site without spending a penny!

Q. Can I create a group with my friends to chat with them?


Ans. This is not a social networking site my friend! Groups created here are the college clubs which are created as per the rules laid by the institute

Q. Is this the only feature you have?


Ans. Don't under-estimate us! We Provide other useful features too. Check at our FEATURES page.

Q. I have another doubt which is not on this FAQ page. What shall I do?


Ans. Don't worry! Just send us your query at iiitd.finance.management@gmail.com. We are more than happy to solve your doubt.
